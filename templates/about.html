Personal

After growing up in the Washington DC suburbs I've moved around a bit, living in the Chicago area, Tucson, and now Denver.

Professional
I am presently a software developer at Rebellion Defense. I have degrees in mathematics (CNU) and computer science (OSU). My personal interests lean toward exploring methods in scientific computing (particularly cryptography, numerical analysis, among other topics) and learning new programming languages. Previously I've worked at Wolfram Research, helping build the scientific computing software Mathematica, and GEOST, working on high-powered telescopes and optical systems to keep track of artificial satellites in earth's orbit.
